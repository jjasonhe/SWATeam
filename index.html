What is NYLT?

A 6-day course that teaches practical leadership skills for Scouting and beyond.

Participants arrive and are grouped into patrols of 6 to 8 scouts, led by a trained Troop Guide. The course simulates a month in a scout unit: participants learn various leadership and scout skills, culminating in a hike and overnight camping trip with their patrol.
